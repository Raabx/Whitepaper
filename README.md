# RAABX

**Roll-ups As A Business**  
**Bitcoin-Cadence Infrastructure for Real-World Business Operations**

RAABX is a **Bitcoin-first, Bitcoin-ready** protocol designed to give businesses a full on-chain operating backbone with deterministic finality, audit-grade receipts, zero-PII compliance, always-earning staking, and modular Business L3 infrastructure.

Today, RAABX operates as a **Bitcoin-anchored Layer 2** with fixed-cadence anchoring and explicit sealing semantics. Over time, it is designed to support a **BTC-only covenant-enforced path** as Bitcoin scripting evolves.

RAABX is built for organizations that need:

## Protocol Highlights

### Bitcoin-Anchored L2
RAABX posts fixed-cadence commitments to Bitcoin, producing tamper-evident receipts and a durable audit trail.

### Deterministic Business Finality
RAABX introduces explicit timing controls for business workflows:

- **Auto Seal** for standard block cadence
- **Instant Seal** for near-immediate publication
- **Cut-off Seal** for scope-level accounting closure without halting other activity

### Public / Private Lane Design
RAABX separates customer-facing activity from confidential internal operations while preserving shared proof integrity.

### Zero-PII Compliance
Compliance is handled through zk-KYC proofs, revocation freshness rules, and policy-flexible predicates without putting personal data on-chain.

### Always-Earning Security Model
With Universal auto-Delegation & Staking (UDS), balances participate in securing the network by default while operator quality and health remain central to rewards.

### Business-Grade Product Stack
RAABX extends beyond settlement into operational infrastructure, including:

- Passport & Profile
- Commons
- Business Launchpad
- Dev Studio
- Token Library
- OpenTax
- Router
- BusinessNet
